Standardized Furniture Concept (2023)

← →

My project focuses on the idea of developing a furniture series based on standard DIN components such as Euro perforated panels and C-rails.
This approach not only enables considerable cost savings, but also creates a modular and durable furniture collection.
By using standardized parts, the furniture is easy to rebuild and new configurations can be easily extended.

Tram Concept Regensburg (2022)

Experimental concept together with the city of Regensburg

← →

Design can create a balance between vehicle, architectural elements and the urban environment. We gathered impressions from Ratisbona and were inspired by the cityscape. Various typical buildings and their materiality led us to the frame construction inside the Parametric Tram.

Riot Furniture Concept (2023)

Taken Objects Furniture Series

← →

Riot Furniture is a concept merging furniture design with architectural and urban elements to make a bold statement. Exhibited at M26 in Regensburg alongside industrial design bachelor theses and other works , the series draws on the raw aesthetics of resistance. With materials like steel, construction side elements, each piece resembles urban barricades and structures, symbolizing resilience and the interplay between design and public space.
