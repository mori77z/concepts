Standardized Furniture Concept (2023)

A furniture series based on standard DIN components such as Euro perforated panels and C-rails. This approach not only enables considerable cost savings, but also creates a modular and durable furniture collection by using standardized parts, the furniture is easy to rebuild and new configurations can be made.
